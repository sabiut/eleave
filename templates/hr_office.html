    • Add new staff

  • 1. From the left menu under staff infomation, click on Add new staff.

Generating current Leave Balance

1)Click on the leave info link from the left hand side menu.This will take you to the list of all the staff who have apply for the leave.
2) Click on the leave calculator check box for the staff that you want to generate his/her current leave balance, This will take you to the leave calculator form.
3) Fill up all the necessary fields and then click on calculate current balance
4) The eLeave system should now generate the current leave balance of the staff that you have selected and store the current leave balance on the backend database.
