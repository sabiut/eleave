  • Leave Application Guide
  • 1. Under Leave Application, Click on Application Form.
  • 2. You should now see the leave application form once you click on the Application Form.
  • 3. Fill out all the nessary fields on the leave application form, then click on Apply for leave button at the bottom.Follow the next screen to complete your leave application.
  • 4. Please note that on the department field, fill out your department field with this format: ACSD,GOV,CSD,FISD,RSD,FMKD.Only pick department that involves you.
  • 5. The start date and end date field, should be in this format: Year-Month-Day eg:2015-2-15
  • 6. In the Total working days field, Please exclude Holidays and Weekends

  • Leave Authorization Guide
  • 1. Once you have received leave notification from email, Login in to .
  • 2. If you are the department head, click on the department head link and then click on the department that involves you. Then click on the leave applicant to approve the leave. The department head authorization field should be in the format: Approved or in the format: Rejected Authorization date should be in the format: Year-Month-Day eg:2015-2-15.
  • 3. If you are the department Director, click on the Coporate Services link and then click on the department that involves you. Then click on the leave applicant to approve the leave. The Corporate services authorization field should be in the format: Approved or in the format: Rejected Authorization date should be in the format: Year-Month-Day eg:2015-2-15.
  • 4. The Leave Applicant will be notify automatically via email once the department head and the department director approves the leave.
  • 5. The eLeave system will automatically calculate the current leave balance and store it in the database.
